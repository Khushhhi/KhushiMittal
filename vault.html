"Until I feared I would lose it, I never loved to read. One does not love breathing." ― Harper Lee

Some books, TV shows, movies, blogs, articles, and people have had a profound impact on me. From learning how to think to feeling strongly for totally fictional characters i'll never meet--these are the part of my life that I truly treasure.

The best of all time

  • The Story of Your Life and Others by Ted Chiang
  • I, Robot series by Isaac Asimov
  • Mossad: The Greatest Missions of the Israeli Secret Service by Michael Bar-Zohar, Nissim Mishal
  • The Greatest Minds and Ideas of All Times by Will Durant
  • Dark Matter by Blake Crouch

Books that make me feel

  • Forty Rules of Love by Elif Shafak
  • Anxious People by Fredrick Backmann
  • Man's Search for Meaning by Viktor E. Frankl
  • On Earth We're Briefly Gorgeous by Ocean Vuong (writes beautiful poetry)
  • The Heart is a Lonely Hunter by Cardon McCullers
